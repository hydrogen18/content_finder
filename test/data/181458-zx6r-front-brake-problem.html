check pads for Foreign Objects, you may have picked something up on your last ride. my pads always made some noise.
make sure your pads are not cocked and the pistons are working correctly. you might have to clean or rebuild your calipers.
you may not have got all the air out when you bleed the breaks last.
you might want to try speed bleeders or a pump to bleed your breaks also.
